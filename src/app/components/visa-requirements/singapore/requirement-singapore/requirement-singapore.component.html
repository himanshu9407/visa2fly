TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Singapore

Passport

Passport

When you apply for a singapore e visa for indians, you will require a passport that is valid for the next 6 months from the date of entry into Singapore is required. Its biodata page must be photocopied.

Photograph

Photograph

Your recent passport-sized color photographs must be submitted. These must have been taken within the last three months. The photo must be 35mm wide by 45mm high, without any borders, and taken with a white background.

Bank Statements

Bank Statements

Bank statements updated for the last 6 months are necessary, to show you have sufficient funding to last your entire visit to Singapore. These bank statements must be duly attested and signed by a bank official.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

Flight tickets are necessary for your entry into Singapore. Of this, the most important is a confirmed return ticket you will have to furnish when you apply for a singapore e visa online.

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

A cover letter that states your purpose, duration and itinerary of stay is required and must be shown upon demand. This letter must also include contact and expense details of the traveler.

Additional Documents

Additional Documents

If the passenger has been invited for anything, they must fill an additional form V39. This must be sent along with an invitation letter. In the case of sponsorship, proof of sponsorship and financials of the sponsor (may include written and signed consent - a sponsorship letter) must be shown. If you are self-employed, proof of a partnership deed or business registration is required.
