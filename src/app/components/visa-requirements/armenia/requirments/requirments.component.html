TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR ARMENIA

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

The application form should be completed in full and submitted. Please make sure it is completed in black ink and block letters. It must also be signed by the applicant and this signature needs to match the one on the applicantâ€™s passport.

Photograph

Photograph

You must submit photographs that are passport-sized and of International Passport Photo guidelines.

  • Include white background
  • Photo size must be 45mm high and 35 mm wide (lxb=45x35)
  • The head must cover 80% of the photograph
  • Applicant has to provide recent colored photo which must not be older than 6 months.
  • Keep your expressions neutral; remember your face should be clearly visible!

    Flight Tickets

    Proof of flight bookings, both to and fro must be shown. That includes any stops in-between.

    Passport

    Passport

    Your passport should be valid for at least 30 days after your intended departure date from Armenia. It must also have at least 2 unused (blank) visa pages. The applicantâ€™s passport should be valid during the whole period of stay in the Republic of Armenia.

    Hotel Reservation

    Hotel Reservation

    Proof of your confirmed hotel reservation must be shown. This could be rental agreements, hotel reservations, as well as invitations to reside in home (if a known person is inviting you to Armenia).

    Residence Permit

    Residence Permit

    You must be able to show proof of residence in your home country. This could be in the form of ID proof that states your address proof.

    Invitation Letter(visiting family)

    Invitation Letter(visiting family)

    If you are coming to visit family, the invitation letter must be issued from your host in Armenia. It must state the address of the host and passport details of the host and applicant.

    Invitation Letter(business trip)

    Invitation Letter(business trip)

    An invitation letter from your host company in Armenia must be produced, if you are going on a business trip. This letter should be on the companyâ€™s official letterhead and include details of the authorized signatory such as their passport details and the companyâ€™s address.

    Proof Of Financial Stability

    Proof Of Financial Stability

    You must be able to show that you can bear all expenses for your trip to Armenia. This could include bank statements for the last three months (that are duly stamped and signed by the bank), receipts of a grant/study financing, a sponsorâ€™s letter, or any other document confirming availability of financial funds.

    Travel and Health Insurance

    Travel and Health Insurance

    You must be able to show both travel and health insurance for the entire validity period of the visa.
