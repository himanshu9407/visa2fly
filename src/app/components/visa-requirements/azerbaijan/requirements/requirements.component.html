TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Azerbaijan

Passport

Passport

You must hold a passport valid for at least 3 months after your intended date of travel.

Photograph

Photograph

A recent color passport-size photograph must be submitted. This must be taken on a white or cream-colored background. A neutral expression must be maintained.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

Confirmed round-trip tickets must be shown upon application of the visa. Please note, a return ticket is mandatory.

Proof Of Sufficient Funds

Proof Of Sufficient Funds

Sufficient funding is required to guarantee entry into Azerbaijan, and proof of the same must be displayed (in the form of hard cash, or bank statements).

BUSINESS VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Azerbaijan

Passport

Passport

You must hold a passport valid for at least 3 months after your intended date of travel.

Photograph

Photograph

A recent color passport-size photograph must be submitted along with your Azerbaijan e-visa application. This must be taken on a white or cream-colored background. A neutral expression must be maintained.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

Confirmed round-trip tickets must be shown upon application of the visa. Please note, a return ticket is mandatory when you apply Azerbaijan e-visa online.

Proof Of Sufficient Funds

Proof Of Sufficient Funds

Sufficient funding is required to guarantee entry into Azerbaijan, and proof of the same must be displayed (in the form of hard cash, or bank statements).

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

An invitation letter, either by the host company in Azerbaijan or the sponsoring company in your current county, must be shown. It should contain the name and passport details of the traveler, as well as the official sign/stamp of the company. It must be addressed to the respective authorities of the Republic of Azerbaijan.
