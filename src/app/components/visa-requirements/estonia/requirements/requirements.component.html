TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR ESTONIA

Travel Document/Passport

Travel Document/Passport

A travel document should be submitted as well. It must be issued less than 10 years ago, with at least two blank pages present. It should be valid for at least 3 months longer than the date on which you intend to leave the Schengen Area. You will need to submit the original as well as one photocopy.

Application Form

Application Form

You will need to complete the application form for a Estonia visa, with the date and your signature. It should be printed and returned in original form when the application is filed.

For Minors

For Minors

Those applicants who are under the age of 18 need to present their school card as well as an NOC from their school that states the permission for absence, and the name and function of the person granting permission. There will need to be a photocopy of this document as well.

Travel Health Insurance

Travel Health Insurance

Proof of Travel medical insurance must also be submitted to obtain your visa. This should be a copy of the applicantâ€™s travel medical insurance policy, with a coverage of not less than 30,000 euros. It should cover medical, hospitalisation expenses for the entire period of valid stay, as well as repatriation costs.

Flight Reservations

Flight Reservations

You will need to show a roundtrip flight reservation that includes any third country. This does not need to be a confirmed flight ticket, a reservation is enough.

Itinerary

Itinerary

You will need to show proof of your reasons for travel and your travel plans. This is in the form of a travel plan/ itinerary for your entire duration of stay.

Hotel Accommodation

Hotel Accommodation

You will need to show proof of accommodation as well. This could be in the form of all hotel reservations for your entire stay (in the Schengen area).

Proof of Solvency

Proof of Solvency

The applicant needs to show a proof of solvency in the form of a proof of pension or other regular income (for retired people). For employees, a letter on the official company paper with stamp, signature, date and clearly mentioning:- address, telephone and fax numbers of the employing company-the name and position in the employing company of the countersigning officer-the name of the applicant, position, salary-and years of service-approval for leave or absence is needed. For unemployed adults - if theyâ€™re married, a letter of employment and income of the spouse and a apostilled certificate of marriage is required. If single/divorced/widowed: you can show any other proof of regular income.

Proof of Funding

Proof of Funding

You will need to show your personal income and whether this will be enough to fund your trip to Estonia, using supporting documents. These could be a copy of bank statements of the last 6 months. You could also show the past two yearsâ€™ ITR-V forms or your last 3 salary slips as proof.

Business Licence

Business Licence

Your companies business licence (on official company paper with stamp and signature) and an original letter from your employer is required. The letter should include the full address and contact persons of the company- which includes the name and position of the countersigning officer. The applicantsâ€™ name, position, salary and years of employment should also be mentioned. The purpose of the visit, including confirmation of position after the return, is also required. the person or the entity who will bear your travel and living costs (if it is the company) should be mentioned.

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

An original invitation letter on the official company paper with stamp and signature should be submitted. It should mention the full address and contacts of the company, the name and position of the countersigning officer, the purpose and duration of the visit, as well as a detailed program of the details. The person or the entity who will bear your travel and living costs should also be mentioned, as well as whether the sponsor gives financial guarantee for your return to India.
