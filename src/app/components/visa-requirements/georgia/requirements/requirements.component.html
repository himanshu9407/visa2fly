TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR GEORGIA

Application Form

Application Form

Visa application form that is completed and signed by the applicant is necessary. If the applicant is a minor, the form must be signed by both the legal guardian and the minor.

Photograph

Photograph

This is a digital copy of your passport/ passport-sized photograph. It should be a recent colour copy, with 472 x 610 pixels in dimension. If scanned, it must be 4 x 5.16 cm in size and scanned at 300 DPI resolutions.

Passport

Passport

This passport must have been issued within the last 10 years. Its validity period should exceed the validity period of the visa by at least three months. It should not be damaged and should not contain unofficial records. Please make sure it has at least 2 blank pages to paste the visa sticker on.

Copy of Tickets

Copy of Tickets

Proof of flight bookings, both to and fro must be shown. That includes any stops in-between.

Hotel Reservation

Hotel Reservation

Proof of your confirmed hotel reservation must be shown. This could be rental agreements, hotel reservations, as well as invitations to reside in home (if known person is inviting you to Georgia).

Proof of Financial Aid

Proof of Financial Aid

You must be able to show that you can bear all expenses for your trip to Georgia. This could include bank statements for the last three months (that are duly stamped and signed by the bank), receipts of a grant/study financing, a sponsor’s letter, or any other document confirming availability of financial funds.

Travel and Health Insurance

Travel and Health Insurance

You must be able to show both travel and health insurance for the entire validity period of the visa.

Itinerary

Itinerary

You should be able to show proof of a document that confirms the purpose of your stay in Georgia. This, for a tourist purpose, could be an itinerary.

Letter of Invitation

Letter of Invitation

This letter must be issued by the person currently residing in Georgia, and must be written in either Georgian or English. The name and surname of the invited person must be written only in English, with Latin transliteration.
