If you want to extend your visa, you will need to go to the nearest Provincial Directorate of Migration Management in Turkey (once you reach). There, you will have to apply for a residence permit. If you overstay your visa without applying for this, you are liable to pay fines, be deported or even banned from future travel into Turkey.
