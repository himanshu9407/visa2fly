TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR EGYPT

Form

Form

You will also need to provide a photocopy of a completely filled visa application form. This must be signed as well.

Passport

Passport

Your passport will need to have a validity of at least 6 months after your intended departure date from Egypt.

Photograph

Photograph

Two colour photographs are required. You must send them along with your application form. They must be passport-sized, and meet the standards for an international passport photo.

Proof of Funds

Proof of Funds

You will need to show that you have enough money to last your stay in Egypt. This can be in the form of salary slips for last 6 months (if employed), or bank statements of the last 6 months.

Statement Of Purpose

Statement Of Purpose

You will need to provide a covering letter from the applicant that states the purpose and duration of the visit. This letter must include the passport details of the applicant (full name, passport number)

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

You will need to submit proof of leaving Egypt after your business is finished. This must be in the form of original confirmed return/onward air tickets.

Hotel Reservation

Hotel Reservation

A copy of your hotel reservation details for your entire stay is also required.

Income Tax Return, PanCard

Income Tax Return, PanCard

An original and photocopy of the applicant’s Income Tax Returns of the last 3 years is required. A photocopy of their pan card is also required.

Character Certificate

Character Certificate

You may be asked to submit a Character certificate. This is to ensure you have no criminal record, and must be issued from the local police of the home country (India).

Travel Insurance

Travel Insurance

You will need to show that you have sufficient travel insurance to cover any losses.

Business Registration Proof

Business Registration Proof

If you own a business, or if you are going as part of a company, you will need to show proof of the legitimacy of the business. This is in the form of the company’s business registration.

Memorandum of Article (MOA)

Memorandum of Article (MOA)

The MOA of the company must also be shown in some cases. This may be asked of you.

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

You will need a copy of the invitation from your company. This is the host company in Egypt. They will need to send it on a company letterhead, as well as have all of your passport and employment details.

Registration Details

Registration Details

If you are going to Egypt for a conference/event, you will need to show the registration details of the same.
