If you come under any of the following categories, you will not need to obtain a transit visa:

  • Diplomatic Passport holders
  • If you hold a valid visa/residence permit from a member state of the Schengen area or Bulgaria, Romania, Croatia, Cyprus, Liechtenstein, Ireland, UK, Canada, Japan, USA(except for holders of an US “Advanced Parole Document”)
  • If you hold a valid residence permit for Andorra, Monaco or San Marino
