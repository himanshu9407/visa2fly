TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR GERMANY

Application Form

Application Form

You will need to complete the application form for a Germany visa, with the date and your signature.

Travel Document/Passport

Travel Document/Passport

A travel document should be submitted as well. It must be issued less than 10 years ago, with at least two blank pages present. It should be valid for at least 3 months longer than the date on which you intend to leave the Schengen

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

You will need a personal covering letter that includes a day-to-day itinerary and your purpose of travel. Make sure to include your passport number and full name as well.

Hotel Accommodation

Hotel Accommodation

You will need to show proof of accommodation as well. This could either be a hotel reservation for your entire stay, or confirmed and paid advance at your accommodation. This will need to be for the entire Schengen area: all places of accommodation must be shown.

Flight Reservation

Flight Reservation

Attach a copy of your flight tickets or reservations to the supporting documents. Please include return tickets as well.

NOC / Leave Letter

NOC / Leave Letter

You will need a leave letter (if employed) from your company stating that you are free to travel. For students, you will need an NOC (No Objection Certificate) from your institution.

Health Insurance

Health Insurance

Proof of health insurance must also be submitted to obtain your visa. This should be a copy of the applicant’s health insurance policy, with a coverage of not less than 30,000 euros. It should cover medical, hospitalisation expenses for the entire period of valid stay, as well as repatriation costs.

Passport Data Page

Passport Data Page

You will need one copy of your passport’s data page on an A4 sized paper.

Proof of Financial Status

Proof of Financial Status

You will need to show proof that you are able to fund your entire trip in Germany. This can be shown via the following documents. If you are employed, you can submit payslips of the past three months or your employment contract. If you are self-employed, you can submit a Certificate of Proprietorship or another proof of ownership (e.g. proof of land title, proof of income from agriculture (sales form) etc.). Both employed and self-employed people will have to submit their personal original bank statements for the past three months with proof that it contains enough funding to cover the stay in Germany. Also, the Income Tax Return (ITR) form or Form 16 (Certificate of Income Tax deducted at the source of salary) must be submitted.

Proof of Civil Status

Proof of Civil Status

You will also need to show proof of civil status. This can be in the form of a marriage certificate, birth certificate (for children), death certificate of spouse, or a ration card if applicable.

Photograph

Photograph

You will need to provide two passport-sized ID photographs, according to the biometric specifications. Please make sure that your photo is taken within the last 6 months.

For Minors

For Minors

If minors are travelling unacccompanied by their parents, a consent letter must be submitted. This letter should state that the parent consents to this decision (of the minor travelling), and it should be signed by both parents as well as the applicant.

Invitation Letter
