TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR CANADA

Credit/Debit Card

Credit/Debit Card

You need a valid credit or debit card to make the visa fee payment. Please note, visa offices do not accept payment receipts from Canadian banks (assuming you are applying from outside Canada).

Biometrics

Biometrics

You will need to submit biometrics for the visa to be processed. You will generally pay the biometrics fee before you get your biometrics processed.

CIC Forms

CIC Forms

You will need to submit 3 forms: the Application for Temporary Resident (IMM 5257), Family Information form (submit by those older than 18, Statutory Declaration of Common-Law Union (IMM 5409). There are 2 other forms - the IMM 5476 and IMM 5475 - that might have to be submitted in certain cases.

Passport

Passport

You will need to submit a photocopy of the information page of your passport. This should include your photo, name, DOB, passport issuance and expiry dates and the passport number. Your passport should be valid for your entire intended stay.

Photograph

Photograph

You will need 2 photos that meet the Canadian photo requirements. On the back of both photos, write your name and date of birth. If you are providing biometric, you don’t need physical copies of your photos.

Proof of Financial Support

Proof of Financial Support

You must include proof that you are able to support yourself and your family who are travelling on this trip.

Marriage License/Certification

Marriage License/Certification

You will need to show a photocopy of your marriage license or certification.

Purpose of Travel

Purpose of Travel

You will need to show your purpose of travel in the form of an itinerary and invitation letter.

Current Immigration Status

Current Immigration Status

You will need to show proof of your current immigration status as a photocopy. If your country of residence is different from your country of citizenship, you will need to show proof of legal status in your country of residence.

For Minors

For Minors

Minors will need to provide custody documents/letter of authorization by other non-travelling parent (if travelling with only one parent) or letter of authorization signed by both parents/legal guardians (if travelling alone).
