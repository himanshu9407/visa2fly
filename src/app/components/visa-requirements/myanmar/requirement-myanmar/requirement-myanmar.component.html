TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR MYANMAR

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

You will need to complete and sign an e-visa application form, which will be submitted at least 7 days before you leave to Myanmar.

Photograph

Photograph

One recent colour photograph must be submitted. The dimensions for these photos are 4.6cm x 3.8 cm, and must be taken within the last 3 months.

Passport

Passport

This document must be valid for at least 6 months after the date of entry into Myanmar.

Credit Card

Credit Card

You must be able to pay the visa fees online with this credit card. Only Visa, Master, American Express , JCB credit cards or Alipay are accepted.

Company Registration

    Onward Visa
