TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR UZBEKISTAN

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

You will need to fill an application form online, on the official Uzbekistan e-visa application page.

Photograph

Photograph

You would need 2 passport-size photos with white or grey background with the dimensions of 35X45mm.More details are mentioned in the FAQ section below.You will need a passport-sized photograph available as well. Please include a digital copy, as you might be asked to submit that along with your application.

Credit Card

Credit Card

You will be asked to pay the visa application fees online, for which you will need a accessable method of online payment, i.e. a credit/debit card.

Passport

Passport

For this application form, you will need your passport, valid for at least 6 months before entry into the country. You will also need a scanned copy of both the first and last pages, which they will ask you to submit on the form.

BUSINESS VISA REQUIREMENT FOR UZBEKISTAN

Passport

Passport

For this application form, you will need your passport, valid for at least 6 months before entry into the country. You will also need a scanned copy of both the first and last pages, which they will ask you to submit on the form.

Photograph

Photograph

You will need a passport-sized photograph available as well. Please include a digital copy, as you might be asked to submit that along with your application.

Application Form

Application Form

You will need to fill an application form online, on the official Uzbekistan e-visa application page.

Employement Letter

Employement Letter

An employment letter from your company stating that you are currently employed with them is needed. It must be on the official letterhead and signed as well.

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

The company in Uzbekistan that you will be going to, will need to submit a letter saying that you will be joining them. Make sure that the date and personal details are matching that of your passport.

Flight Ticket

Flight Ticket

If you are obtaining a business visa on-arrival, you will need to submit proof of your flight tickets as well.
