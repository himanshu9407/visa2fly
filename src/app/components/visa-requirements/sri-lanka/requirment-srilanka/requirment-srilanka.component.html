Completed ETA Form

Completed ETA Form

This form requires personal and passport information. This includes contact details, full name, passport number, issue, and expiry date, as well as the country of birth and citizenship.

These documents are enough to obtain an ETA. However, upon arrival, the below documents must be shown to guarantee entry into Sri Lanka:

Printed Copy of ETA

Regarding your Sri Lanka E-visa online, a printed copy of the approved ETA must be shown at the Immigration checkpoint.

Proof of Sufficient Funding

You must be able to guarantee that you are financially independent. This may be in the form of bank account statements, or any other acceptable form of proof.

Flight Tickets

Flight tickets, especially return tickets, must be displayed upon asking. This could be a return ticket to your resident country or an onward ticket to a third country.
