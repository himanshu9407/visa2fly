TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR MALTA

Application Form

Application Form

Kindly fill in the application form for the visa, and duly sign it as well.

Photograph

Photograph

You will need to submit a passport-sized photograph. This should be in colour with a white background and a size of 3.5*4.5cm.

Passport

Passport

You will need to submit your passport/travel document. This should be valid for at least 3 months after your date of departure FROM Malta. It should also be issued within the last 10 years and have at least 3 blank pages.

Medical Insurance

Medical Insurance

Medical insurance for a Schengen visa is a must. Please ensure you have valid overseas medical insurance, if not, contact Visa2Fly immediately. The insurance should cover a cost over or equivalent to 30,000 Euros.It should also cover the entire duration of the trip (including dates of arrival and departure).

Travel Itinerary

Travel Itinerary

You must have a confirmed travel itinerary which includes your dates of travel. If you are staying for less than 90 days, please include return flights as well.

Proof of Accomodation

Proof of Accomodation

These can be shown in two ways. Either a document displaying a confirmed hotel booking, or the ‘Declaration of Proof’ having been completely filled, signed (by the invitee, along with their Malta ID card or passport) and submitted will suffice. All the latter documents must be in original, witnessed by (Maltese) Public Notary.

Proof of Funding

Proof of Funding

You should be able to prove that you have sufficient funds to sustain your trip. This can be proven in the form of salary slips or a cover letter from the bank (including stamped and signed bank statements of the last 6 months)

Aadhar Card

Passport Pages

Along with the current passport, you will need to provide the original and photocopy of all the pages of the current and previous passport(s) of yours.

Aadhar Card

Aadhar Card

You will also need to provide your Aadhar Card for the visa application process - along with a photocopy.

Economic Status of Company

Economic Status of Company

This must be proved by documentation: the economic status of your company/institution/individual in Malta, to whom you are going to do business with. This can be in the form of a) a copy of the company’s Registration Certificate, b) Bank Statements of the company in the last 3 months, or c) The ITR accompanied by the verified CPC acknowledgement for the last 3 years.

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

A letter must be issued by the inviting company. This should explain the details of activities/business relations with the Indian company, and also the professional status and other particulars of the applicants. Along with these, the purpose and dates of visit must also be included.
