TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR ANTIGUA & BARBUDA

Passport

Passport

You will need to send over your bio page of a valid passport or travel document. The passport needs to have a validity of a minimum of 6 months after your arrival in Antigua and Barbuda.

Photograph

Photograph

There are some specifications for your photograph. It needs to be a recent color passport photograph that is 45mm x 35mm. All your photos should be on a white background only, and should not be altered or edited from its original state.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

You will need evidence of the journey into and out of Antigua and Barbuda. This can be shown in the form of ongoing+return tickets, or a confirmation of your booking from the travel agent.

Accommodation

Accommodation

This accommodation proof needs to be for the whole length of your stay. If you have someone you are staying with (i.e. accommodation is in someone’s house) you will need a letter of invitation from them.

Proof Of Funds

Proof Of Funds

You will need to show proof of funds to finance your whole trip. This can be in the form of bank statements for the last two months.

Police Record

Police Record

You may need to provide a recent police record to prove that your intentions to travel are sound. Discretion may be taken by the visa-issuing office regarding this.

Birth Certificate

Birth Certificate

You will need to provide a birth certificate of the applicant. If you have children and they are applying, you will need a birth certificate along with an NOC (No-Objection Certificate that states the guardians give permission for the minors to travel.)

Residency Permit

Residency Permit

You must be able to show proof of residence in your home country. This could be in the form of ID proof that states your address proof. This is to show your immigration status, and must match with the one in your passport.

Travel Itinerary

Travel Itinerary

The itinerary talks about the plan of your trip, from day-to-day and for the entire stay in Antigua and Barbuda. This is a document you will need if you want to apply for the visa.
