The stay period is the maximum amount of time that you will be able to stay in Tajikistan for. In contrast, the validity is how long your visa is valid for. This means that if your visa is valid for 3 months, you can go and stay in Tajikistan for any 60 days in those 3 months. Therefore, you cannot stay in Tajikistan for 90 days - that is the validity and not the stay period.
