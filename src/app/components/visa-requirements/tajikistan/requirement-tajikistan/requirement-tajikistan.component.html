Visa Application Form

Photograph

Photograph

Hotel Reservation

Hotel Reservation

Proof of your confirmed hotel reservation must be shown.

Passport

Passport

For this application form, you will need your passport, valid for at least 6 months before entry into the country. You will also need a scanned copy of both the first and last pages, which they will ask you to submit on the form.

Flight Ticket

Flight Ticket

Proof of flight bookings, both to and fro tmust be shown. That includes any stops in-between.

Proof Of Funding

Proof Of Funding

Proof of sufficient funds for the duration of their stay. This can be in the form of income tax returns of the last three years, or salary slips of the last three months. If retired, you would need to show your retirement proof in the form of a pension book etc.
