TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Switzerland

Form

Form

When you apply for a Swiss visa online, the applicant needs to fill up an online form that will require details such as Personal, Travel, Passport, Dependant, Parents, Employment, Income and expenditure, Accommodation, Travel history (Schengen and other areas) and other histories.

Don’t worry! V2F agent will come to your doorstep and assist you in filling up the complete form as per the information provided by you.

Passport

Passport

Passport details are mandatory. The passport must be issued within the last 10 (ten) years and with a minimum of 3 months' validity after the scheduled return, with a minimum of two blank pages. Previous Passports (if any) should be bound alongside an elastic band (not stapled) and sent alongside the appliance.

Photograph

Photograph

Two (2) passport size photographs are required. Specifications - White background, not older than 6 months (size 35 – 40 mm in breadth, not copied or scanned picture).

Proof of Funds

Proof of Funds

Note - All documents have to be originals, in format A4, with stamp and signature of Bank.

If employed :

Proof of employment: employment agreement & last 3 months salary slips & last 3 months salary checking account statements.

Personal ITR (only ITR-V, Indian tax Return Verification Form or Acknowledgement is accepted) for the last 2 assessment years.

If self-employed and/or company owner:

Enterprise registration certificate / Partnership Deed / Proof of proprietorship, or other proof of ownership.

Last 3 months personal & business checking account statements.

Company’s & personal ITR (only ITR-V, Indian tax Return Verification Form or Acknowledgement is accepted) for the last 2 assessment years.

If retired :

Personal pension statement for the last 3 months.

Persons who apply for a Swiss e-visa online must remain in Switzerland at their own expense must be ready to demonstrate their financial resources amounting to a minimum of CHF 100 per day / Rs. 7500 per day of their stay.

Eg - 2 people traveling together for 10 days are advised to demonstrate a minimum of 2 x 10 x 7500 = Rs. 1,50,000/- in available funds.

If Student or unemployed :

A student who applies for a Swiss e-visa online, Their parents or statutory supervisors must present their personal bank statements for the latest 3 months.

Individual ITR (only ITR-V, Indian tax Return Confirmation Form or Acknowledgement is admitted) for the latest 2 assessment years along with a confirmation letter of financial support and a duplicate of their passports.

Accommodation

Accommodation

Proof of accommodation mentioning where you'll be staying during your entire stay within the UK, which might be at a lover or relative’s place or at a hotel or a hostel.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

Factual roundtrip flight program for visa application or confirmed flight reservation.

Itinerary

Itinerary

Your detailed travel itinerary which provides information about your travel plans in Switzerland/Schengen Area alongside tickets, places you're getting to visit, your meeting agenda, tours booked beforehand, your festival agenda, etc.

Employment / Student Proof

Employment / Student Proof

For Employed Persons:

For Employed Persons - An introduction letter from the employer is required on the company/business letterhead. the first letter must be signed & stamped by HR or directorate mentioning your position, duration of your service, the date and purpose of the trip.

For Self-Employed Persons –

Business registration documents confirming the business owner’s name and therefore the date the business started trading and three years of tax returns of the business.

For Students –

A letter from your institution head, on headed paper, with your enrolment confirmation and leave of absence.

Families -

When you apply for a Swiss visa online, The visa form needs to be submitted together, only the top member of the family must attach bank statements, personal ITR (only ITR-V, Indian tax Return Verification Form or Acknowledgement is accepted), travel insurance (mentioning all the names of the insured), flight booking (mentioning all the names of the traveling family members), all the hotel confirmations (if all the rooms are under an equivalent booking and under the name of the top of the family).

Minors -

Students above 16 years:

Copy of the scholar college ID card and introduction letter of the School/College/University.

• Children under 18 years old, traveling alone :

written consent letter of both parents or trustees, certified by a public notary.

• Children under 18 years old, not traveling with both parents :

written consent letter of the opposite, non-traveling parent or trustee, certified by a public notary.

Other Documents

Other Document Requirements when you apply for a Swiss Visa Online:

1. If the applicant continues to visit a 3rd country (i.e. UK, USA, Canada etc…) after leaving the Schengen Area, the visa of the subsequent country has got to be obtained first.

2. Clear photocopies of valid UK, USA, or CAN visas (if any).

3. If you've got been refused a visa by an Embassy or High Commission within the last 2 years, please provide the Refusal Notice or a written explanation about the explanations for refusal.

Travel Insurance

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is mandatory. The minimal coverage of 30,000 Euro / 50,000 USD / 50,000 CHF per individual. Coverage of all expenses which could arise in reference to repatriation for medical reasons, urgent medical attention and/or emergency hospital treatment. The insurance must cover the whole stay within the Schengen territory. Applicants who wish to use for 6 months or one-year multiple entry visas aren't obliged to supply a 180 (365) days coverage. it's sufficient to submit a policy covering the primary journey.

Applicants applying for a period of 6 months or one-year multiple-entry visas are not entitled to provide 180/365 day coverage. Submitting an insurance policy that covers the first journey is sufficient.

Visit our travel insurance section (link) to book your travel insurance instantly.

Personal Covering Letter

Personal Covering Letter

Original letter with an itinerary. If the applicant is submitting the applications of his/her family, then all applications must include a replica of the duvet letter.

IMPORTANT VISA INFORMATION FOR Switzerland

  • All Indian passport holders require a valid visa to enter Switzerland.

  • Each applicant must have all the mandatory documents as mentioned in requirement tabs below

  • In addition to the documents mentioned, our visa experts or the concerned embassy may require you to furnish additional documents to process your visa.Your cooperation in furnishing any such additional documents is essential for a positive outcome.

  • If all the documents are not ready at the time of agent visit then additional charges levied when agent visits your residence again

  • Fee may vary for country if rate of the embassy fluctuate.

    • FAQ's

    As a general rule, a choice is taken by the Consulate within 15 days. this era may, in individual cases, be extended.

    Please note these are the processing times after your Biometrics are completed. Ideally, it's advised to start out your Swiss e-visa online application process 6-8 weeks prior to your intended date of departure.

    The decision to refuse a Schengen visa and therefore the reasons for the refusal are notified to the applicant employing a standard form. You will get the chance to appeal within 30 days of receipt of the refusal. For information on appeal procedures, please ask the webpage of the State Secretariat for Migration.

    Make sure to give a minimum time of 6 months before you apply for the visa again.
