Document required for Russia {{ visaType }} visa

Application Form

The online application form must be completed. Although it is available online, you will need to print and submit it offline.

Passport

  • Passport must be issued within 10 years
  • Valid for 6 months beyond the last intended date of departure from this country
  • Should contain at least 2 blank visa pages
  • Should have no alteration or handwritten amendments in the data pages.

Photograph

Two recent passport-sized photographs (45 mm height x 35 mm width), taken against white background.

Accommodation

A confirmation of hotel booking in Russian language is required. If the tour is being arranged by a Travel agency: A letter from the agency for confirmation of arrangements and advance/Payment receipt copy for the same. (Preferable) in Russian language

Business Card

Applicant have to provide Business card if applicant is employed / self employed. In case of student, student identity card must be provided. In any other case you can just inform our agent who will be visiting at your residence to collect document they will prepare the documents accordingly.

Flight Ticket

Only in case of Transit visa applicant must upload both depature and arrival flight tickets.
