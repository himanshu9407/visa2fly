TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR RUSSIA

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

The online application form must be completed. Although it is available online, you will need to print and submit it offline.

Photograph

Photograph

You would need 2 passport-size photos with white or grey background with the dimensions of 35X45mm.More details are mentioned in the FAQ section below.These photographs must be passport-sized, and signed on the back.

Letter Of Confirmation

Letter Of Confirmation

If you are part of a tour group, or are a cruise passenger, you must submit a letter from said tour company or cruise line that displays the itinerary and particulars. You must include a copy of the confirmation from the authorized Russian travel company which shows the reference number and confirmation number for the visa.

Passport

Passport

You must submit your original passport. It should contain at least 2 blank pages for visas, and be valid for a period of 6 months after the visa expires. A good quality copy of the page with photo and address page must also be submitted – both these copies must be on one side of an A4 sheet.

Hotel Arrangements

Hotel Arrangements

The applicant must display a confirmation of hotel arrangements that shows the reference number and confirmation number for the visa. It could be from the official travel company you are going through, or of the actual Russian hotel itself.

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

You will need an invitation letter from a friend, acquaintance, relative, or establishment. The person issuing the letter could be in Russia, or be verifiable as a travel agency that is authorized to issue invitation letters. This letter includes a Tourist Voucher as well as a confirmation of arrival. It is a tourist service agreement that is made between a foreign citizen and a Russian travel company. If you have more questions about this, you may refer to the FAQ’s below.

Itenerary

Itinerary

If your travel length is more than 14 days, then a day-to-day itinerary is also required.

IMPORTANT VISA INFORMATION FOR RUSSIA

  • All Indian passport holders require a valid visa to enter Russia.

  • Applicant must have all the mandatory documents while applying for Russia Visa online as mentioned in requirement tabs below

  • In addition to the documents mentioned, our visa experts or the concerned embassy may require you to furnish additional documents to process your visa. Your cooperation in furnishing any such additional documents is essential for a positive outcome.

  • If all the documents are not ready at the time of agent visit then additional charges levied when agent visits your residence again

  • The fee may vary for the country if the rate of the embassy fluctuates.

    • FAQ's

    It is possible to apply for an e-visa for Russia. However, they are only for three regions (The Far East, St Petersburg and Leningrad region, and Kaliningrad). There are different e-visas for each region, therefore you will not be able to travel to the other regions without a full Russian visa. For a full Russian visa, you will need to go manually, not through an e-visa. The electronic visas are also only available for a short period of time, eg. 8 days.

    If you hold a normal (and not a diplomatic) passport, you will have to apply for a visa before your trip. Visa on arrival is not available for this country as of yet.

    The photograph must be 3.5x4.5cm. It must also be taken no longer than 6 months prior to submitting the application. On the printed application form, you must date and sign it, and then glue this photograph to it. It should be on a light-coloured background, with a clear image of the applicant directly facing the camera.Please make sure the applicant is not wearing dark/ tinted glasses or any kind of headgear. Those who wear said headgear due to their ethnic/religious background are exempted from this rule.

    Yes. You will need an invitation letter from a friend, acquaintance, relative, or establishment. This is necessary for all Russian visas.

    Depending on the type of visa, it can be issued by different people. It can be issued by a friend or a relative staying in Russia or a tourist company/organization. This organization should be accredited at the Department of Consulate Service of the Ministry for Foreign Affairs of the RF or the Federal Migration Service. The travel company must have its own reference number in Russia’s United Register of Tour Operators, so please make sure of this before you sign up.

    Usually, this process can be done in a day or two. You will need to go through a travel agency, who will issue you this letter. This travel company must have its own reference number in Russia’s United Register of Tour Operators, so please make sure of this before you sign up.

    The signature used on the passport, and the one used on the application form must be identical. Otherwise, the application you submit to the Russian Embassy will be considered invalid.

    The online form can be found at this link: visa.kdmid.ru. You will need to fill it out, then print, sign and attach a photograph with it. While completing the application form, you must choose the correct visa application centre which you will be applying to. This is important to note.

    Your letter of invitation will have to contain information on the individual applicant, as well as the company. For the individual, their full name, date of birth, sex, citizenship, phone number and passport details must be submitted. Their duration and purpose of visit, number of entries granted by the visa must also be shown. If applicable, the names and dates of birth of minor children accompanying the person invited must also be shown. For the hosting company (who is issuing the invitation), the full name of the organization, its registered and actual addresses, its individual taxpayer identification number and state registration number must be provided. The first name, surname and designation of the person signing the invitation, along with their contact telephone number and the organization’s official seal is also necessary.

    The letter can be any of the following:

  • Should be issued by the Federal Migration Service of the Russian Federation; or the Ministry of Internal Affairs of the Russian Federation. These must be originals, unless they are issued electronically - then a copy will suffice.
  • Or, it could be a visa directive sent by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation
  • Or, you could submit a direct letter of invitation from the host organization (a copy can be submitted)

    • Business visas are used for business purposes, such as attending conferences, signing papers, etc. You can also partake in tourist purposes, but that should not be your purpose of travel. Please note, you will not be able to work a job with this visa, it is temporary.

    There is one speciality to be noted - you will need to obtain your business invitation letter directly from the inviting company in Russia (INN Letter), or have a letter of invitation that is issued by a Main Directorate for Migration Affairs in Russia. You must also have been to Russia on a business visa (single or double) in the last one year.
