TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR BELGIUM

Travel Document/Passport

Travel Document/Passport

A travel document should be submitted as well. It must be issued less than 10 years ago, with at least two blank pages present. It should be valid for at least 3 months longer than the date on which you intend to leave the Schengen Area. You will need to submit the original as well as one photocopy.

Application Form

Application Form

You will need to complete the application form for a Belgium visa, with the date and your signature. It should be printed and returned in original form when the application is filed.

Photograph

Photograph

You will need to provide a passport-size coloured ID photograph, according to the biometric specifications. Please make sure it is not older than 6 months. This will need to be glued onto your application form. Size of photograph should be 35mmx45mm.

Previous Visas

Previous Visas

A copy of the ID page of your passport along with your most recent Schengen visa (if any) including stamps will need to be submitted.

Itinerary

Itinerary

You will need to show proof of your reasons for travel and your travel plans. This is in the form of a travel plan/ itinerary for your entire duration of stay.

Hotel Accommodation

Hotel Accommodation

You will need to show proof of accommodation as well. This could be in the form of all hotel reservations for your entire stay (in the Schengen area).

NOC / Leave Letter

NOC / Leave Letter

A letter from your employer that states your income and your vacation days allowed should be submitted. Or, in the case of students, a certificate from your institute stating your vacation days allowed should be submitted.

Proof of Funding

Proof of Funding

You will need to show your personal income using supporting documents. These could be a copy of bank statements of the last 3 months. You could also show the past two years’ ITR-V forms or your last 3 salary slips as proof.

For Minors

For Minors

Minor applicants will need to submit 3 more documents. The passport and ID copies of both parents are required, along with a notarized birth certificate of the minor. If the parents/ a parent is not travelling with the child, a notarized letter stating the parents’ / parent who is not travelling does consent to this decision and permission to travel is given. This document needs to be apostilled.

Travel Health Insurance

Travel Health Insurance

Proof of Travel medical insurance must also be submitted to obtain your visa. This should be a copy of the applicant’s travel medical insurance policy, with a coverage of not less than 30,000 euros. It should cover medical, hospitalisation expenses for the entire period of valid stay, as well as repatriation costs.

Flight Reservations

Flight Reservations

You will need to show a roundtrip flight reservation that includes any third country. This does not need to be a confirmed flight ticket, a reservation is enough.

Passport

Passport

This document must be valid for at least 6 months after the date of entry into Belgium.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

You must have proof of ongoing flight tickets to your next journey (after transiting from Belgium).

Onward Travel Visa

Onward Travel Visa

You will also need the visas (if applicable) for your next destination (the one you will go to after transiting from Belgium).
