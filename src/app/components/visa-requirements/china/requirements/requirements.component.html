TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR China

Application form

Application form

The applicant will require to fill up a China visa online form that will require details such as personal information, travel information, and other extra information.

Don’t worry! When you apply for China visa online, an agent from Visa2Fly will come to your doorstep and assist you in understanding the entire process.

Passport

Passport

Passport details are mandatory. The applicant passport must have at least 6 months remaining validity, available blank pages and a photocopy of the passport's data page.

Photograph

Photograph

Chinese visa requires a recent passport-size color photo with a white background. (48mm X 33mm)

Hotel Reservation

Hotel Reservation

Proof of hotel reservation

Flight ticket

Flight ticket

Proof of your Flight Ticket booking

Itinerary

Itinerary

When you apply for China visa online, your detailed travel itinerary gives information about your travel plans in China along with tickets, Places you are going to visit, Your meeting agenda, Tours booked in advance, Your festival agenda, etc.

Invitation Letter from China

Invitation Letter from China

Submit the invitation letter that was issued by the inviter (whether it’s an entity or any individual) with the following details

Applicant’s full name, Date of birth, Passport number etc.

Intended business visit, Date of arrival, Date of departure, Purpose, Place(s) to be visited

Inviter’s name, Contact number, Address, Official stamp, Signature, etc.

Other Supporting Documents

Other Supporting Documents

If you are not applying in your country of citizenship, proof of legal stay or residence status will be required when you apply for China visa online.

In some cases, the applicant might have to submit a certification letter from the employer with the following details :

Name and Contact number of the Employer

The Income Statement of the Applicant, or

Original copy of the 6-month Bank Statement of the Applicant.

Applicants intending to travel to Tibet can obtain a Tibet Travel Permit from the Tourism Bureau of Tibet Autonomous Region.
