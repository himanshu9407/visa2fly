The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland consists of 4 countries, England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland and 14 overseas territories. The UK is mainly known all over the world due to its history of the British Empire, its colonies and other literary, visual arts and cinematic influences. Classics like Harry Potter and James Bond originated here too! The UK is the world’s 10 th biggest tourist destination with over 37 million people visiting the country in 2018 alone. It is home to over 25 World Heritage Sites and has some of the most stunning landscapes and historical architecture. London is the most visited city in the UK and Edinburg, Oxford, Cambridge, York and Canterbury are some other popular tourist cities. The UK’s geographical and cultural diversity is another factor that attracts millions of tourists here every year. The 4 countries have their unique landscapes and histories which greatly influence the lifestyle of the people and the tourist attractions too. The castles, estates, royal residences, art galleries, museums are just some of the exciting things you can expect here.
