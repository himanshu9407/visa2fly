TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR UGANDA

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

You will need to fill up the application form online, with includes particulars such as your passport details, accommodation details, travel history, etc.

Photograph

Photograph

You would need 2 passport-size photos with white or grey background with the dimensions of 35X45mm.You will need a digital copy of your most recent passport-sized photographs. These must be taken within a month of submission.

Hotel Reservation

Hotel Reservation

You will need to show proof of a hotel reservation, as the application form online asks for the address of your accommodation.

Passport

Passport

You will need a digital copy of your passport. Both the biodata pages must be submitted clearly in the copy.

Yellow Fever Vaccine

Yellow Fever Vaccine

You will need to show proof (the Yellow Fever Card) of having been injected with the yellow fever vaccine recently.

Passport

Passport

Recommendation Letter

Recommendation Letter

A recommendation letter from a referee in Uganda (eg. from your company in Uganda) is required to further prove your case of needing a visa permitting multiple entries instead of a single entry. You will need one recommendation letter for a 6-month validity visa and 2 for a 12-month/24-month validity.

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

You will need to submit a cover letter that states your reasons for requiring a multiple entry visa.

Police Clearence

Police Clearence

You will need a valid police clearance from Interpol or your home country.

Previous Entries

Previous Entries

You will need proof of all your entries and exits into/out of Uganda in the last 1 year. This is necessary to show that you will need a multiple entry visa instead of a single entry.

Passport

Photograph

Yellow Fever Vaccine

Letter

Letter

You will need a letter that states your reason for transit, as well as your country of destination.

Destination Visa

Destination Visa
