Visa Application Form

Hey pals, this is going to be the first step right? Yeah, for sure. So, firstly fill the application form by visiting the official website of embassy. It may ask for various details such as:-

  • Personal
  • Travelled earlier to Rwanda or not!
  • Passport
  • Employment
  • Bank Details
  • Income
  • Expenditure and may be a lot more

    • Keep calm and fill it patiently. If in trouble, just need to contact us and our agent will be at you doorsteps to help you in filling form.

    Photograph

    Photograph

    Rwanda visa photo requirements are mentioned below:-

  • Include white background
  • Photo size must be 45mm high and 35 mm wide (lxb=45x35)
  • The head must cover 80% of the photograph
  • Applicant has to provide recent colored photo which must not be older than 6 months.
  • Keep your expressions neutral; remember your face should be clearly visible!
    •

    Cover Letter

    Cover Letter

    Pals, Cover letter is the first thing embassy checks. So, your cover letter needs to be at its best! It must contain:-

  • Each and every detail of schedule from date of visit to leaving the destination
  • Place of stay
  • Passport details
  • Plans after visiting Rwanda
  • Expenses
  • Travel itinerary etc
    •

    Passport

    Passport

    As it’s known, Passport being the most important part of whole process. It is having a validity of at least 6 months and must have three blank pages in order to be able to be able to attach visa. It has to be clean and neatly maintained.

    Flight Ticket

    Flight Ticket

    You will need proof for confirmation of your flight tickets. It contains your flight and destination details. Also keep copies of tickets in case you need them!

    Accommodation

    Accommodation

    So, during this beautiful trip you’re going to stay somewhere right? Therefore, you will have to give the proof of your stay (booking details or invitation letter by relatives/friend). Which could be any place you like, it must be a hotel your friends or relatives place!

    Itinerary

    Itinerary

    Travel itinerary is just the overall summary of your travel plans. So with respect to this you have to mention travel details, date of travel and schedule of your plan (timings).

    Proof Of Funds

    Proof Of Funds

    Travelling comes with a package! i.e. you need to have a big bank balance. Also, you need to provide bank statements for past 3 months. Take prints of your bank statements and attach it to the file.

    Yello Fever vaccination

    Yello Fever vaccination

    This is a certificate where it provides proof of Yellow fever. Make sure you issue it from an authorized medical organization/hospital.
