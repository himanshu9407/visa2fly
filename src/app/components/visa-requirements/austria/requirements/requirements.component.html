TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR AUSTRIA

Application Form

Application Form

Kindly fill in the application form for the visa, and duly sign it as well.

Passport

Passport

The validity of your passport should be more than 3 months from when you are planning to depart from Austria. It needs to contain 2 blank passport pages (at least), and have been issued in the past 10 years. The data page should be clean and legible, with no handwritten altercations or any other alterations.

Along with the current passport information pages, you will need to provide photocopies of all the pages of the current passport of yours.

You will need to submit originals of your previous passports, or show proof of their loss.

Photographs

Photographs

There should be 2 photographs of the applicant attached. These must be in accordance with the international standards that are set out in the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO). Size of photographs should be 35mmx45mm.

Return Visa/Permit

Return Visa/Permit

You should have a valid return permit to any country you are entering after leaving from Austria (including your home country). This can be shown as a Residency Permit or a return visa.

Medical Insurance

Medical Insurance

You should have a valid travel medical insurance which is valid throughout the entire Schengen territory. The minimum coverage of the insurance should be EUR 30,000.

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

You should be in possession of a cover letter that explains the purpose of the trip. This should include an itinerary with detailed location(s) of your travel and the duration of your stay in Austria.

Occupational Information

Occupational Information

You will need to show evidence of your occupation/student status. Please check the FAQs below to know more about the exact documents required to be submitted.

ITR

ITR

You will need to submit your Income Tax Acknowledgement: ITR-V for the last 3 year’s.

Proof of Sufficient Funding

Proof of Sufficient Funding

You will have to show that you have sufficient funds to travel. These can be submitted in the form of original bank statements of the last three (3) months and/or other funds available (such as pension).

Return Flight Tickets

Return Flight Tickets

When applying for the visa, you will need to show round-trip flight tickets for airline reservation. You will also need to show proof of how you will travel within Austria/EU. You can compile both these details in a travel itinerary which you can submit as one document.

Proof of Accommodation

Proof of Accommodation

You will need to provide proof of accommodation through the means of a hotel reservation in Austria. You will also need, in the case of travel to other EU states, reservations there as well.

TRANSIT VISA REQUIREMENT FOR AUSTRIA

Passport

Passport

This document must be valid for at least 6 months after the date of entry into Austria.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

You must have proof of ongoing flight tickets to your next journey (after transiting from Austria).

Onward Travel Visa

Onward Travel Visa

You will also need the visas (if applicable) for your next destination (the one you will go to after transiting from Austria).
