TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR UKRAINE

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

Visa application form that is completed and signed by the applicant is necessary. If the applicant is a minor, the form must be signed by both the legal guardian and the minor.

Photograph

Photograph

The photograph must be 35*45mm in size. It should be coloured and not more than 6 months old. Your mouth must be closed, and the background should be on a white/light coloured background.

Travel and Health Insurance

Travel and Health Insurance

You must be able to show both travel and health insurance for the entire validity period of the visa. It needs to cover the entirety of Ukraine and for the required amount - a minimum of â‚¬30 000 or equivalent coverage.

Passport

Passport

This passport must have been issued within the last 10 years. Its validity period should exceed the validity period of the visa by at least three months. It should not be damaged and should not contain unofficial records. Please make sure it has at least 2 blank pages to paste the visa sticker on.

Flight Ticket

Flight Ticket

Proof of flight bookings, both to and fro tmust be shown. That includes any stops in-between. It must be booked before applying for a visa, and must include the name and surname of applicants.

Residence Permit

Residence Permit

You must be able to show proof of residence in your home country. This could be in the form of ID proof that states your address proof. This is to show your immigration status, and must match with the one in your passport.

Proof of Financial

Proof of Financial

You must be able to show that you can bear all expenses for your trip to Ukraine.

Proof Of Tourism

Proof Of Tourism

You will need to show proof that you have come to Ukraine for the main purpose of tourism. These can be shown in certain documents: including accommodation booking with complete details of all applicants, a programme of stay (i.e. itinerary), information on the tour including tour timeframes, tourist agency details, transfer details, sightseeing information etc.

Supporting Documents

Supporting Documents

You will need 2 passport photocopies, including any pages that have a previous Ukraine, USA, UK, Schengen or Australia visa.
