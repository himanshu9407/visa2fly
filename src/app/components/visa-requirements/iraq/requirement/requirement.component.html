TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR IRAQ

Passport

Passport

Your passport will need to have a validity for a minimum of 6 months from your date of arrival into Iraq.

Application Form

Application Form

You will need to submit your original application form. It must be duly filled and signed by the applicant.

Photograph

Photograph

You will need two colour photographs of yourself/ the applicant. It should fulfil the international passport photo requirements, and be taken within the last 6 months.

Sufficient Funds

Sufficient Funds

You will need to show evidence of sufficient funds to cover your cost of living while staying in Iraq. This can be in the form of the last 3 month’s bank statements.

Itinerary

Itinerary

You will need to provide a convincing reason for your visit to Iraq. This could be in the form of an itinerary, which allows you to show your tourist plans and where you will be travelling. Make sure to include flight details as well as dates.

Iraq Address

Iraq Address

You will need the full address of the place where you will be staying in Iraq. It will need to be filled in Arabic..

Health Certificate

Health Certificate

In the case that your child is below the age of 15, a health certificate is required. This is to prove a full polio vaccine round has been administered and the child is vaccinated for at least 5 doses of oral polio vaccine.
