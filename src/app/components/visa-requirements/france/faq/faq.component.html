FAQ's

As a general rule, a decision is taken by the Consulate within 20 days. This period may, in individual cases, be extended.

Please note these are the processing times after your Biometrics have been completed. Ideally, it is advised to start your visa application process 6-8 weeks prior to your intended date of departure.

Any decision on the part of refusal of a visa to the applicant shall be notified using a standard form citing the reasons. You can appeal within the first 30 days of receipt of the visa refusal.

If you are not updated with the reason for visa denial, you can apply for the visa again after a period of 6 months.
