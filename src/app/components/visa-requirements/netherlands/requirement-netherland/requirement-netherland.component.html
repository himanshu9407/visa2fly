A passport valid for at least 3 months after your departure date (from the Netherlands) is required for your visa. It must have at least 2 empty visa pages, and cannot have been issued more than 10 years ago. You require a copy of all the pages of your passport as well.

Marriage Certificate

Proof Of Financial

Proof Of Financial

An original private bank statement showing all movements of funds for the past 3 months must be submitted. Income tax return acknowledgments for the last two years must also be submitted.

Depending on your employment category, you must submit additional documents: If employed, payslips for the last 3 months, an employment contract, and an employer's statement for approval to travel must be submitted. If you are sponsored, a separate Proof of Sponsorship form must be submitted along with the netherlands visa online for indians. If you are retired, pension statements for the last 3 months or proof of property/business ownership (generation of regular income) must be shown.
