TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR NETHERLANDS

Form

Form

You will need to complete a netherlands visa online for indians form.Don’t worry! A V2F expert will do that on your behalf and guide you every step of the way.

Passport

Passport

A passport valid for at least 3 months after your departure date (from the Netherlands) is required for your visa. It must have at least 2 empty visa pages, and cannot have been issued more than 10 years ago. You require a copy of all the pages of your passport as well.

Evidence of Legal Residence

Evidence of Legal Residence

You must provide evidence of legal residency in the country you are applying from (in this case, India). This could be in the form of a visa or residence permit and a copy of a work permit (if applicable). In the case of submitting residence permits, please make sure they are valid for at least 3 months after departure from any of the Schengen countries.

Photograph

Photograph

A photo of the applicant (not taken more than 6 months ago) must be along with your netherlands visa online for indians. Please see the FAQs at the bottom of the page for photo guidelines to be followed.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

Round tickets (both onward and return) for your trip are required. Please make sure these are refundable, in case your visa request is refused.

Proof Of Lodging

Proof Of Lodging

Hotel bookings (or other arrangements for lodging, such as hostel reservations) for your entire stay in the Netherlands must be shown.

Itinerary

Itinerary

A certificate of the travel agency that confirms the booking (of an organized trip, if you are traveling in a group) or any other appropriate document that indicates the travel plans must be submitted along with netherlands visa online for indians.

Marriage Certificate

Marriage Certificate

If you are married and do not have proof of the same on your passport, then you must display a marriage certificate, or a copy of your ration card if applicable.

Proof Of Financial

Proof Of Financial

An original private bank statement showing all movements of funds for the past 3 months must be submitted. Income tax return acknowledgments for the last two years must also be submitted.

Depending on your employment category, you must submit additional documents: If employed, payslips for the last 3 months, an employment contract, and an employer's statement for approval to travel must be submitted. If you own a business, a certificate of registration of your company must be shown. If you are sponsored, a separate Proof of Sponsorship form must be submitted along with the netherlands visa online for indians. If you are retired, pension statements for the last 3 months or proof of property/business ownership (generation of regular income) must be shown.

Health Insurance

Health Insurance

Health insurance that has been taken in your name, and which is valid throughout the Schengen area and for the entire time of your stay must be submitted. At least € 30,000 of medical costs should be able to be reimbursed.
