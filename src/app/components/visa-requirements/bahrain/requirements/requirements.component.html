TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR BAHRAIN

Passport

Passport

Your passport should be photocopied. In addition, the digital photocopy of the last page must also be submitted.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

You will require a copy of a return air ticket for after your stay in Bahrain.

Hotel Booking

Hotel Booking

Your copy of a hotel booking in Bahrain must be submitted. Alternatively, in case you are staying with a relative, you should provide a copy of their CPR Reader’s printout.

Bank Statements

Bank Statements

A copy of a stamped bank statement under the visitor’s name must be submitted. It should cover the last three months, with an ending balance of minimum BD 300.

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

The Indian individual must issue a cover letter that mentions the purpose of visit, designation of the visitor, and the intended duration, place and days of stay. It must also contain the individual’s passport details. In case of not being able to obtain a cover letter, other documents that prove valid objectives for travelling to Bahrain must be shown, eg. itinerary.

Supporting Document

Supporting Document

If applicable, you may attach a copy of a GCC/USA/Schengen/UK/European/Canadian visa obtained that is still valid.

PhotoGraph
