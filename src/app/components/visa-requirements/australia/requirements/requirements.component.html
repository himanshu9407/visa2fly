TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR AUSTRALIA

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

When you apply for an Australia e-visa online, this form needs to be filled up. Don’t worry, a visa2fly expert will fill it up for you and guide you every step of the way.

Photograph

Photograph

You would need 2 passport-size photos with white or grey background with the dimensions of 35X45mm.More details are mentioned in the FAQ section below.

Aadhar/Pan Card

Aadhar/Pan Card

Your Aadhaar or PAN card must be photocopied and submitted.

Passport

Passport

Your current passport, with a minimum of six months’ validity (from the date of departure from Australia) must be submitted. All of your old passports must also be shown. All passports must also be notarized and photocopied.

Bank Statements

Bank Statements

Original bank statements for the last 6 months are required to be presented - this is to show that you have a stable and sufficient balance for the trip.

Hotel Reservation

Hotel Reservation

A copy of your hotel reservations for the entirety of your trip is required.

Employment Documents

Employment Documents

Depending on your state of employment, you will be required to prepare and display certain documents when you apply for an Australia e-visa online. Please check the FAQs at the bottom of the page for more information on what to supply.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

these are confirmed return flight tickets to your home country/ the country from which you traveled to Australia. A travel itinerary can also be presented instead of this - either way, you would need to book return flight tickets for both options.

TRANSIT VISA REQUIREMENT FOR AUSTRALIA

Passport

Passport

Your current passport, with a minimum of six months’ validity (from the date of departure from Australia) must be submitted when you apply for an Australia E-visa online. All of your old passports must also be shown.

Supporting Documents

Supporting Documents

These may include tax returns, a travel itinerary, proof of funds (in the form of bank statements, copy of passbook, any other proof of cash), PAN card, or a business card (if you have one).

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

Visas and flight tickets to your next destination (after transit) are required. Please make sure you have these BEFORE you apply for a transit visa.
