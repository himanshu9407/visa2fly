TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR ZAMBIA

Visa Application Form

Visa Application Form

Those who are applying for an e-visa will need to first create an account on the official visa page (we can assist you in doing so). Then, a Form 16 (Application For Visa) must be filled out.

Photograph

Photograph

You would need 2 passport-size photos with white or grey background with the dimensions of 35X45mm.More details are mentioned in the FAQ section below.A passport-size photo of the applicant is necessary. Since this is an e-visa, please make sure this is in a digital format.

Accomodation

Accomodation

You, as a visitor (in Zambia), must have and be able to produce full information on your host, and provide a physical address where you will be staying for the entire duration of your stay.

Passport

Passport

A passport-size photo of the applicant is necessary. Since this is an e-visa, please make sure this is in a digital format.

Flight Ticket

Flight Ticket

Those who arrive in Zambia by air (which is most of the Indian population) must be in possession of valid return tickets. Upon showing proof of the same, you will be allowed into the country.

Credit/Debit Card

Credit/Debit Card

After completing and reviewing the form, you will be directed to an online banking site to pay the visa fees. Please make sure that you have enough balance in your cards to facilitate this amount.

BUSINESS VISA REQUIREMENT FOR ZAMBIA

Passport

Passport

You must be in possession of a valid passport, with at least 3 fully empty pages. It must be valid for 6 months after the date of your travel

Photograph

Photograph

You must keep 2 passport-sized photos of you handy, it will come in necessary when filling the forms at the Zambia airport.

Accomodation

Accomodation

You, as a visitor (in Zambia), must have and be able to produce full information on your host, and provide a physical address where you will be staying for the entire duration of your stay.

Business Registration

Business Registration

The proof of a registered business of the inviting company must be printed. It should be shown to the Immigration POfficer if asked.

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

A cover letter from your home company must be shown. It must be on the official letterhead, as well as stating your personal details and travel purpose.

Flight Ticket

Flight Ticket

Those who arrive in Zambia by air (which is most of the Indian population) must be in possession of valid return tickets. Upon showing proof of the same, you will be allowed into the country.

Sufficient Funds

Sufficient Funds

Those who arrive by land or water must show proof of sufficient funding to cover both their stay in Zambia, as well as their return to the respective country.
