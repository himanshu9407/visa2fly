TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR SOUTH AFRICA

Form

Form

The application form-11 (DHA-84) should be completed in full and submitted. Please make sure it is completed in black ink and block letters. It must also be signed by the applicant and this signature needs to match the one on the applicant’s passport.

Passport

Passport

Your passport should be valid for at least 30 days after your intended departure date from South Africa. It must also have at least 2 unused (blank) visa pages. You should also carry copies of your passport biodata page. If you have any old passports, you will need to provide those as well. It is mandatory if you are applying in Mumbai,Pune,Ahmedabad,Goa and Bangalore.

Photograph

Photograph

You will need 2 recent photos. These should be less than 1 month old and must have dimensions of the International Passport size.

Proof of Funds

Proof of Funds

You will need to prove that there is enough funds for you to be able to travel in South Africa. This may include the last 3 month’s bank statements. These statements should be signed and stamped by the respective bank. Passbooks, e-statements and net banking copies are not accepted. Your bank statement must be showing your account having a balance of South African Currency R 3000 (or equivalent in INR).

Hotel Booking

Hotel Booking

Before you travel and apply for a visa, you need to provide proof of hotel booking, which should be verifiable. This means it should state the name and passport number of the applicant(s). The duration of visit, physical address, and contact details of the hotel should also be mentioned in this proof. The proof must have an authorized signature with their name and designation.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

You will need to show proof of a valid flight ticket, or provide proof of a flight reservation. This must be for both onward and return trips.

Itinerary

Itinerary

You will also need a day-to-day itinerary/travel plan for your whole stay in South Africa.

No Objection Certificate

No Objection Certificate

An NOC must be submitted from the applicant’s employer (if employed)/ school/ university (if studying). It must clearly mention the name and passport number of the applicant, as well as their duration of visit. This letter must have the postal address of the institution, as well as contact details of the authorized signature’s (i.e. employer or institutions’) name and designation. If you are travelling with a minor/the minor is travelling alone, you will need to show an NOC from their legal guardians stating the same.

Yellow Fever Vaccination Certificate

Yellow Fever Vaccination Certificate

This is necessary only if the applicant is travelling through/staying in the yellow fever belt of South America/Africa before coming to South Africa.

Marriage Certificate

Marriage Certificate

If married and wherever applicable, you will need to provide your original Marriage certificate for verification purposes.

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

You must provide an original covering letter that is signed by the applicant. It should state the name and passport number of applicant(s), as well as their duration of visit. This letter should have the postal address, as well as contact details of the applicant - their name, designation and signature is also necessary. In the letter please clearly mention who will bear the expenses of the trip.

Invitation Letter

A letter of invitation from the South African organizers/hosts/companies must be submitted. This should state the applicant’s name and passport number, as well as the purpose and duration of visit. It must also have the company’s physical/postal address, and the contact details of the host as well. Regarding the host, it must have theirs (or another authorized party’s) name, designation and signature. The host’s ID copy (attested by commissioner of OATHS) should be submitted if required by SAHC.

Flight Tickets

You will need to show proof of a valid flight ticket, or provide proof of a flight reservation. This must be for both onward and return trips.

Introduction Letter

This letter should contain the physical address, as well as contact details of an authorized person (at company), along with their designation and signature.

TRANSIT VISA REQUIREMENT FOR SOUTH AFRICA

Passport

Passport

You must have a valid passport that stays valid for 6 months after your designated travel to the destination country

Onward Ticket

Onward Ticket

You must be able to produce onward tickets to your destination country.

Visa Requirement

Visa Requirement

If the destination country requires a visa, you must be able to produce this if asked.
