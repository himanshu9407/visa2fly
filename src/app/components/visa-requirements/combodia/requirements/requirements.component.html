TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Cambodia

Passport

Passport

When you apply for a cambodia e visa online, you require a passport that is valid for the next 6 months from the date of entry into Singapore. Its biodata page must be photocopied.

Photograph

Photograph

Your recent passport-sized color photographs must be submitted. These must have been taken within the last three months. The photo must be 35mm wide by 45mm high, without any borders, and taken with a white background. The photographs must be submitted in JPEG or PNG format.

Debit/Credit Card

Debit/Credit Card

A valid debit/credit card is required for making the visa fee payment. For which reason, please make sure yours has more than/equivalent to the required amount.
