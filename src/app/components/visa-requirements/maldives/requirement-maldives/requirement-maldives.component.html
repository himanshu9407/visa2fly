TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Maldives

Passport

Passport

Passport details are mandatory when you apply for a Maldives E-visa. A passport or a travel document with Machine Readable Zone (MRZ) is required, with at least 6 months validity beyond the date of departure from the country. It should also contain 2 blank pages.

Hotel Booking /Accommodation Proof

Hotel Booking/Accommodation Proof

A pre-paid hotel booking/accommodation must be shown and indicated on the Disembarkation Card.

Foreign Exchange

Foreign Exchange

Sufficient funds to last for the intended period of stay in the Maldives is necessary, this is usually calculated as USD100 plus USD 50 per day.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

A valid credit/debit card is required for making visa payments, as well as to display proof of financial independence.

Completed Disembarkation Card

Completed Disembarkation Card

The Disembarkation Card is available both on the flight and as soon as you land. You must fill it up completely and truthfully. It contains particulars such as name (in full; as it appears on passport), nationality, country and date of birth, passport details, place of residence, mode of arrival, the purpose of visit, etc.

Yellow-Fever Vaccination Certificate

Yellow-Fever Vaccination Certificate

When you apply for a Malaysia E-visa, those from countries that have a high risk of yellow fever must produce their yellow fever vaccination card on arrival.

BUSINESS VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Maldives

Passport

Passport

Passport details are mandatory, in case you apply for Maldives E-visa Online. A passport or a travel document with Machine Readable Zone (MRZ) is required, with at least 6 months validity beyond the date of departure from the country. The passport Bio-data page must be scanned, in color copy, and submitted in PDF format.

Business Visa Application Form

Business Visa Application Form

This is the form of IM24. It must be submitted in PDF format.

Registration Copy

Registration Copy

A Registration copy of the applicant must be submitted in PDF format.

Diploma Qualification Proof/Letter of Experience

Diploma Qualification Proof/Letter of Experience

You should hold at least a diploma qualification, and proof of the same. If not, a letter from your overseas company or entity may be presented, stating your capability, as well as the purpose of the visit. This may be attached along with any other certificates you may have. This must be in PDF format.

Photograph

Photograph

A 2 x 2-inch photo with a white/cream-colored background must be submitted in JPEG format.

Copy of Expatriate Medical Insurance Policy Schedule

Copy of Expatriate Medical Insurance Policy Schedule

This must also be a PDF file.

Letter from Sponsor

Letter from Sponsor

This letter must state the foreigner’s (you) period of stay in the Maldives, among other things such as the job position. It must be in PDF format.

Visa Applicant Form

Visa Applicant Form

A filled copy of the Visa Applicant For, if applicable, must be submitted. This form is named IM 30 and must be attached in PDF format.
