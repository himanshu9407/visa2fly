Carrying a large bulk of items (eg. food ingredients, sweets, cultural items, etc.) may attract a $400 fine. There is a certain amount of each that is allowed, please ensure you carry only that amount. Fresh or packaged food, fruit, eggs, meat, spices, pulses, plants, seeds, skins and feathers are not allowed. The full list of items is available at: https://www.newzealand.com/assets/cc0f74822b/MPB0132-Indian-DoD-1-pager-v8.pdf.
