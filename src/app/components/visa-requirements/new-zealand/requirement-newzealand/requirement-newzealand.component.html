TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR NEW ZEALAND

Passport

Passport

You will need a valid passport or travel document to show proof of identity. This could either be a passport copy (if digital) or the original (if manually). You will have to show proof of any old passport pages as well. Please note, you will need to show all your travel history, not just your biodata pages. Any declined visa applications will also need to be shown.

Photograph

Photograph

You must show a passport-size photo online (if you apply for an e-visa) or two actual passport-sized photos (if applying manually).

Flight Ticket

Flight Ticket

This is a travel ticket to a country after your stay in New Zealand. It could be your home country, or another third country. The date of departure on this ticket must be before the expected expiration date of your visa.

Sufficient Funding

Sufficient Funding

You must be able to show sufficient funding for your entire stay in New Zealand. If not, you will be at risk of being deported or held back. These could be in the form of bank statements, the last 3 months’ salary slips, etc. In monetary value, this amounts to NZ $1000 per month. You must also agree to pay all your healthcare costs.

Medical Acceptance

Medical Acceptance

You must show proof of a medical x ray/examination to show you are in good enough health to travel.

Proof Of Character

Proof Of Character

You may need to provide proof that you are in good character. This may include any police records that you have.

NOC

NOC

This stands for “No-Objection Certificate”. It may be from your employer (if you are working), or your school/college (if you are studying), or your guardians (if you are a minor). It should state that the institution has no problem with your leave.

TRANSIT VISA REQUIREMENT FOR NEW ZEALAND

Passport

Passport

You will need a valid passport or travel document to show proof of identity. This could either be a passport copy (if digital) or the original (if manually). You will have to show proof of any old passport pages as well. Please note, you will need to show all your travel history, not just your biodata pages. Any declined visa applications will also need to be shown.

Onward Ticket

Onward Ticket

You will need to have onward tickets to your destination country when you apply for the transit visa.

Destination Visa

Destination Visa

You will need to have a visa for the country you will be finally visiting, i.e. the country you will be going to after transit. If this country does not require a visa, then this document is not necessary.

Travel Itinerary

Travel Itinerary

This travel itinerary must show your New Zealand arrival and departure times clearly.
