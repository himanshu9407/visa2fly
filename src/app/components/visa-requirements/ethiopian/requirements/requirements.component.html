There Are Different Type Of {{ visaType }} visa

Conference Visa

  • Duration and Validity: 30 days, validity starts from the intended date of entry to Ethiopia. Conference Visa cannot be extended.
  • Entry: Single entry
  • Fees: 32 USD

Investment Visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry Single (30 days) and Multiple entries ( 90 days, 6 months, 12 months) available
  • Fees: 32, 42, 62 and 122 USD respectively.
  • Extensions are available.

Foreign Business Firm Employment Visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry: Single entry
  • Duration of Stay: 30 days, can apply for an extension before the visa expires.
  • Fees: 42 USD

Ethiopian Government Employment visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry: Single entry
  • Duration of Stay: 30 days. An extension can be requested, before the expiry of the current visa.
  • Fees: 32 USD

Journalist Visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry: Single entry
  • Duration of stay: 30 days, no extension available.
  • Fees: 42 USD

Ethiopian Private Business Firm Work Visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry: Single entry
  • Duration of Stay: 30 days, can apply for an extension before the visa expires.
  • Fees: 32 USD

International Organizations/Embassies Work Visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry: Single entry
  • Duration of Stay: 30 days. An extension can be requested, before the expiry of the current visa.
  • Fees: 32 USD

Government Institutions Short Task Visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry: Single (30 days) and Multiple (90 days, 6 months) entry visas available
  • Fees: 22, 82 and 122 USD respectively.
  • Extensions are available.

NGO Work Visa

  • Processing Time: 3 days
  • Entry:Single entry & Duration: 30 days
  • no extension available
  • Fees: 62 USD

Document required for Ethiopia {{ visaType }} visa

Passport

  • Upload coloured copies of the and of the passport.
  • When you apply for a ethiopia e visa online, you must be in possession of a passport with at least 6 months validity.

Photograph

  • Upload scanned colour copy of your passport-sized photograph
  • should be taken against a white background.
  • Dimensions should be 45mm height x 35mm width.

Residence Proof

Residents of India must also provide valid residence permit in India.

Yellow-Fever Vaccination Card

A yellow fever vaccination card is required for entry into Ethiopia.

Other Document

Indian ETD’s (Emergency Travel Documents) are also accepted.

Support Letter

A support letter/ completion of the application form by the inviting company or nearest Embassy of Ethiopia (depending on visa) must be submitted.

Government Confirmation

A support letter from relevant institution that is part of the Government of Ethiopia (different departments for different visa, e.g. letter from Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Ethiopia for a Conference Visa, letter from Investment Commission of Ethiopia for Investment Visa.) must be shown, along with the previous support letter from the inviting company.

Business/Registration Letter

A Business/ Registration License of the inviting company must be submitted when you apply Ethiopian E-visa online. This is in cases of NGO Work Visa, Ethiopian Private Business Firm Work Visa, Foreign Business Firm Employment Visa, Conference Visa, and Investment Visa.
