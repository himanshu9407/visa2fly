TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR TAIWAN

Application Form

Application Form

You will need to fill in the application form for the ETA online. It is recommended to use a Google Chrome browser to complete this.

Passport

Passport

This document must be valid for at least 6 months after the date of entry into Taiwan. You will be asked to submit a PDF copy of the first and last pages of your passport (the biodata pages).

Photograph

Photograph

You will be asked to submit a JPG copy of a photograph of the applicant. This photograph must be in line with passport size and requirements.

Flight Ticket

Flight Ticket

You will need return tickets to be able to start filling in the application form. Please make sure you are in possession of these before starting your application process.

Credit Card

Credit Card

You will need a credit card to pay visa fees online. This must be a Mastercard or Visa.

BUSINESS VISA REQUIREMENT FOR TAIWAN

Application Form

Application Form

You will need to apply online on the official visa website. Any fields marked with ‘*’ are mandatory, you can choose to leave out any of those that are marked without this ‘*’. We recommend filling all form details. You will also need to sign on it.

Passport

Passport

You will need a passport that will be valid for at least 6 months from your intended date of departure.

Photograph

Photograph

Two passport-sized photos with a white background must be submitted. A minimum of 60% of the photo must be of the applicant’s face.

Proof Of Funds

Proof Of Funds

The applicant needs to provide proof of sufficient funding. This could be in the form of their personal salary account statement for the last 3 months. This statement should have the official, original bank stamp.

Flight Tickets

Flight Tickets

Proof of flight bookings, both to and fro tmust be shown. That includes any stops in-between.

Hotel Reservation

Hotel Reservation

Proof of your confirmed accommodation for your entire stay in Taiwan must be shown. This could be rental agreements, hotel reservations, etc.

Income Tax Return/Form 16

Income Tax Return/Form 16

The applicant’s ITR or Form 16 (either one) for the last year must be supplied.

Registration Certificate

Registration Certificate

A copy of the Indian company’s registration certificate (that is issued by the Indian government) must also be attached.

Credit Card

Credit Card

You will need a credit card to pay the visa fees. Please ensure this card is either MasterCard,Visa or JCB. After paying the fees, you will need to collect the payment slip for submission later.

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

The company/institution in Taiwan must issue a letter that states the purpose of the applicant’s visit, as well as the applicant’s name and passport details. This must be on the official letterhead.

Cover Letter

Cover Letter

The Indian company must issue a cover letter that mentions the purpose of visit, designation of the visitor, and the intended duration and days of stay. If the applicant needs a multiple-entry visa, tentative dates must be mentioned.

Supporting Document

Supporting Document

You will be requested for different documents based on your purpose of visit and nationality. These will be informed to you through email, after you have completed the application form online.

Bank Statement

Bank Statement

The Indian companies bank statements for the last 3 months must also be attached. The original bank stamp must be present on this, and the individual’s ITR for the last 1 year needs to be attached as well.

Proof Of Transaction

Proof Of Transaction

If there is any proof of transaction/trading between the Taiwanese and Indian companies (eg. LC/remittance receipts/business correspondence), these must be submitted as well.
