TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Thailand

Application Form

Application Form

The Thailand visa application form must be duly filled and signed by the traveller.

Passport

Passport

When you apply for a Thailand E-visa online, you must hold a passport valid for at least with an expiration date at least 6 months beyond your intended duration of stay in Thailand.

Photograph

Photograph

2 recent color photographs must be submitted with the dimensions of 35X45mm. These must have been taken in the last 3 months.

Travel Tickets

Travel Tickets

A confirmed return travel ticket must be booked, printed, and submitted to the Embassy, along with all the other required documents.

Bank Statements

Bank Statements

Bank statements of the last six months (original, or attested by the bank) must be shown. Your bank account must have a minimum disposable amount of 700 USD (per person, or 1400 USD per family) at the time of your Thailand visa application.

Hotel Accommodation

Hotel Accommodation

Proof of hotel accommodation, along with the contact number and address of the establishment, must be submitted. In case of any visa problems while in Thailand, the officers will be able to contact you through this channel.

Additional Documents

Additional Documents

If you are traveling for medical purposes, a medical report from the local hospital must be typed out, stating that you require treatment in Thailand. An acceptance letter from the Thailand hospital must also be submitted. Those applying for multiple-entry visas must also show proof of residence (in India).

BUSINESS VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Thailand

Application Form

Application Form

The Thailand visa application form must be duly filled and signed by the traveller.

Passport

Passport

If you want to apply for Thailand E-visa online, You must hold a passport valid for at least with an expiration date at least 6 months beyond your intended duration of stay in Thailand.

Photograph

Photograph

2 recent color photographs must be submitted with the dimensions of 35X45mm. These must have been taken in the last 3 months.

Travel Tickets

Travel Tickets

A confirmed return travel ticket must be booked, printed, and submitted to the Embassy, along with all the other required documents.

Bank Statements

Bank Statements

Bank statements of the last six months (original, or attested by the bank) must be shown. Your bank account must have a minimum disposable amount of 700 USD (per person, or 1400 USD per family) at the time of application.

Hotel Accommodation

Hotel Accommodation

Proof of hotel accommodation, along with the contact number and address of the establishment, must be submitted. In case of any visa problems while in Thailand, the officers will be able to contact you through this channel.

Proof Of Residence

Proof Of Residence

This is for non-Indian residents only.

Invitation Letter

Invitation Letter

An invitation letter from the Thai company that specifies the name of the applicant, the purpose of travel and the period of the meeting/conference must be submitted when you apply for Thailand E-visa online. This letter must be on the company’s letterhead and must indicate the applicant’s name and passport details with a valid date.

Letter From Indian Company

Letter From Indian Company

A letter from the Indian company which contains the company's profile, and which confirms the applicant's current designation in the company, purpose of travel and period of travel must be submitted. This letter must be on the company’s letterhead and must indicate the applicant’s name and passport details with a valid date.

Registration Papers

Registration Papers

These are registration papers of the Thai company, with a list of shareholders that have been issued no more than 6 months prior. These are also called DBD Papers.

Tax

Tax

These are the Thai company’s business & income tax papers.
