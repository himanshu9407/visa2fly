Document required for Thailand {{ visaType }} visa

Passport

E-VOA :
  • Upload coloured copies of the and of the passport.
  • Your passport must be valid for at least 30 days from the date of arrival and have at least 1 page available for Visa Stamp.
Sticker :
  • When you apply for a Thailand sticker visa online, you must be in possession of a passport with at least 6 months validity with two blank pages.

Photograph

E-VOA :
  • Upload scanned color copy of your passport-sized photograph.
  • should be taken against a white background.
  • Dimensions should be 45mm height x 35mm width.
Sticker :
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs (45 mm height x 35 mm width), taken against white background. These must have been taken in the last 3 months.

Flight Ticket

E-VOA :
  • Upload confirmed air ticket showing round trip for no longer than 15 days.
Sticker :
  • Confirmed return travel tickets are required.

Bank Statement

E-VOA :
  • Bank statement is required to show that you have sufficient amount support your stay in Thailand (minimum 10,000 THB/person, 20,000 THB/family)
Sticker :
  • Bank statements of the last six months (original, or attested by the bank) must be shown. Your bank account must have a minimum disposable amount of 700 USD (per person, or 1400 USD per family) at the time of application.

Hotel Accommodation

E-VOA :
  • Applicant needs to upload their hotel booking voucher / receipt.
  • Your accommodation address in Thailand should be verifiable.
Sticker :
  • Evidence of confirmed hotel/accommodation reservation in Thailand with address and contact number under applicant’s name.
  • If intend to stay with Thai relatives or Thai friends, must provide invitation letter along with a copy of their Thai ID proof
  • In case they are not Thai, they must provide their copies of visa and work permit, valid at least six months along with a copy of passport.

Occupation Proof

E-VOA :
  • Applicant needs to upload his proof of occupation like ID card or NOC from employer mentioning applicant’s name as per passport and designation.
Sticker :

Applicant need to provide proof of occupation and of the monthly income. Applicant can provide documents as mentioned below :

  • Income Tax Returns,
  • Pay slips,
  • Letter from employer certifying employment,
  • Employee ID,
  • Student ID,
  • Business license

Applicants should try to provide as many documents as they can from the given list for a higher rate of success of visa approval.

Proof Of Residence

This is for non-Indian residents only.

Invitation Letter

An invitation letter from the Thai company that specifies the name of the applicant, the purpose of travel and the period of the meeting/conference must be submitted when you apply for Thailand E-visa online. This letter must be on the company’s letterhead and must indicate the applicant’s name and passport details with a valid date.

Company Brochure

Applicant must provide company brochure.
