TOURIST VISA REQUIREMENT FOR Thailand

Application Form

Application Form

The Thailand visa application form must be duly filled and signed by the traveller.

Passport

Passport

When you apply for a Thailand E-visa online, you must hold a passport valid for at least with an expiration date at least 6 months beyond your intended duration of stay in Thailand.

Photograph

Photograph

2 recent color photographs must be submitted with the dimensions of 35X45mm. These must have been taken in the last 3 months.

Travel Tickets

Travel Tickets

A confirmed return travel ticket must be booked, printed, and submitted to the Embassy, along with all the other required documents.

Bank Statements

Bank Statements

Bank statements of the last six months (original, or attested by the bank) must be shown. Your bank account must have a minimum disposable amount of 700 USD (per person, or 1400 USD per family) at the time of your Thailand visa application.

Hotel Accommodation

Hotel Accommodation

Proof of hotel accommodation, along with the contact number and address of the establishment, must be submitted. In case of any visa problems while in Thailand, the officers will be able to contact you through this channel.

Additional Documents

Additional Documents

If you are traveling for medical purposes, a medical report from the local hospital must be typed out, stating that you require treatment in Thailand. An acceptance letter from the Thailand hospital must also be submitted. Those applying for multiple-entry visas must also show proof of residence (in India).

IMPORTANT VISA INFORMATION FOR Thailand

  • All Indian passport holders require a valid visa to enter Thailand.

  • Each applicant must have all the mandatory documents as mentioned in requirement tabs below

  • In addition to the documents mentioned, our visa experts or the concerned embassy may require you to furnish additional documents to process your visa.Your cooperation in furnishing any such additional documents is essential for a positive outcome.

  • If all the documents are not ready at the time of agent visit then additional charges levied when agent visits your residence again

  • Fee may vary for country if rate of the embassy fluctuate.
