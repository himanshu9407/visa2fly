Important Visa Information For Austria
  • Austria is one of the 26 Schengen countries.
  • All Indian passport holders require a valid Schengen visa to visit Austria.
  • If multiple countries in a Schengen area is to be visited then Austria must be the country with the maximum duration of stay or if all the stays at the Schengen countries are same, then it must be the first country at the port of entry in the Schengen area.
  • All Schengen state insist on Bio-metric data collection. Further information available in the section below.
  • The visa processing time is usually q10-15 working days. However, this may vary from case to case.
  • The visa validity period and the number of entries are decided by the visa issuing authority on the case to case basis.
